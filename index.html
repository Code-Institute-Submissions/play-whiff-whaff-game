We present you a Ping-Pong game!

How to play?

  • control the paddle by moving vertically across the left side of the screen (the right side is controlLed by computer)
  • use your paddle to hit the ball back and forth
  • the points are earned when of the players fail to return the ball to the other side
  • stay focused as the ball's speed increases with the number of hits

To close the tips' list click/tap anywhere outside the box!
